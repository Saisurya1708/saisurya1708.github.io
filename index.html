About Me

I'm an Automation & Controls Engineer with 4+ years of hands-on experience designing, commissioning, and optimizing industrial robotics and PLC/SCADA systems in high-volume discrete manufacturing environments.

My work spans the full project lifecycle — from control system architecture and robot programming through FAT/SAT commissioning, operator training, and long-term performance sustainment. I'm equally comfortable at the machine level debugging ladder logic and at the data layer building Python pipelines to surface OEE insights.

What drives me is the intersection of physical engineering and data intelligence — building systems that not only run reliably but get measurably better over time. Every project I deliver has a number attached: throughput gained, defects eliminated, downtime cut.
